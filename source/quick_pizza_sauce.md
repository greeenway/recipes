## Ingredients (for 4 Pizzas)

- 500g tomatoe sauce
- 200g tomatoe paste
- 1,5 TL sugar (9g)
- 1 TL salt (7g)
- 2 TL oregano (6g)
- 1-2 garlic cloves


## Steps
1. Mix all cold ingredients to make the pizza sauce.


## Notes

- EL - tablespoon
- TL - teaspoon
